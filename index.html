Why Cold-Pressed?

Most commercial oils are extracted with high heat and chemical solvents. That process is fast and cheap, and it strips out much of what made the oil worth eating in the first place.

We do it differently. Every oil we sell is pressed slowly on a traditional wooden press (लाकडी घाणी) at low speed, with no heat and no solvents. The oil keeps its natural colour, flavour and nutrients, exactly as the seed held them.

Ansh Enterprises is a family-run store in Deopur, Dhule. We started because our own family wanted food we could trust, and we found others did too. Everything we stock is chosen the same way we choose food for our own table.

Delivery: We deliver within Dhule on orders above ₹500. Orders are confirmed with UPI payment in advance.
